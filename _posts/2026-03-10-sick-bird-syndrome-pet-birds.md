<h2>Why Birds Hide Illness</h2><p>In the wild, a sick bird is a vulnerable bird — predators target the weak. So birds have evolved to mask illness until they physically cannot. By the time your parrot looks sick, it may already be seriously unwell.</p><div class='post-highlight'>🏥 At Shivraj Pet Clinic, we are one of the only clinics in PCMC with dedicated avian veterinary expertise.</div><h2>Early Warning Signs</h2><h3>Behavioural Changes</h3><ul><li><strong>Sitting fluffed up</strong> — puffing feathers signals illness or cold stress</li><li><strong>Sleeping more than usual</strong> — a normally active bird becoming lethargic is a red flag</li><li><strong>Loss of interest in food</strong> — anorexia in birds progresses quickly</li><li><strong>Sitting on cage floor</strong> — healthy birds perch; floor-sitting means significant weakness</li><li><strong>Tail bobbing while breathing</strong> — indicates respiratory effort, a serious sign</li></ul><h3>Physical Signs</h3><ul><li>Discharge from eyes, nostrils or beak</li><li>Change in droppings — colour, consistency, or volume</li><li>Weight loss — bones becoming prominent</li><li>Feather abnormalities — stress bars, blood feathers, excessive moulting</li></ul><div class='post-warning'>⚠️ <strong>Do not wait and watch.</strong> Birds deteriorate rapidly. If you notice 2 or more of these signs, bring your bird to us immediately.
